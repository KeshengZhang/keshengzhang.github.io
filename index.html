Welcome to my NEU homepage. I am currently an associate professor/PhD supervisor in the State Key Laboratory of Synthetical Automation for Process Industries (SAPI), Northeastern University (China). My supervisor is Prof. Tianyou Chai. My current research interests include light field processing, machine learning and working condition recognition in industrial fields.

We are searching for masters and PhDs who are engaged or interested in the above research areas. Those who are interested are encouraged to contact me: wugc@mail.neu.edu.cn.
